Who I am

About

/ Mobile & Full-Stack Engineer

I build cross-platform mobile applications for the Nigerian market — from budgeting apps that read bank alerts in real time to SIP softphones that carry live calls over a PBX. My work sits at the intersection of deep .NET architecture and interfaces people actually enjoy using.

Most of my recent work lives in Xamarin.Forms and .NET MAUI, with custom MVVM foundations, service-locator DI, and platform-specific guards for Android. I also build the web side — ASP.NET Core MVC, Blazor WebAssembly — when a product needs a browser-facing companion.

Based in Abuja, Nigeria, I design for local context first: Naira formatting, Nigerian banking flows, and the network conditions real users face — then engineer it to hold up at production scale.
